The RP Church in Other Countries Canada The Reformed Presbyterian Church of Canada was established as a sister denomination out of the RPCNA in 2023. Their congregations are listed at rpccanada.org . British Isles The RPCNA's story begins in Scotland, and there are many Reformed Presbyterian congregations today in Scotland and Ireland that are part of sister denominations: Reformed Presbyterian Church of Scotland: rpcscotland.org Reformed Presbyterian Church of Ireland: rpc.org Australia You may find churches belonging to our sister denomination in Australia at rpca.org.au .
